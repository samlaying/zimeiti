# CSS内凹边框的核心心法

> 记住“盒子减圆形”这个心法，你就掌握了所有内凹边框技术的核心逻辑。

## 核心内容
实现CSS内凹边框（凹角）的本质几何原理是“从盒子中减去一个位于角落外部的圆形”（box - circle）。所有不同的技术实现，如box-shadow、遮罩、clip-path等，都是对这个减法过程的不同包装和呈现。理解这个核心心法，就能穿透具体技术的表象，抓住实现此类效果的关键。

## 原文关键段落
> but here's the move every technique we're about to learn quietly does underneath you take a circle you place it just outside the corner of your box and you subtract that circle's area away box minus circle that's the whole trick hold that picture in your head as we go box minus circle because every single technique I'm about to show you is just a different scaffolding for doing exactly that subtraction.
